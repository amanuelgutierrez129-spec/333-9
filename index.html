Company Overview

CIT PRO is a professional financial information consulting institution registered in the United Kingdom, founded in 2014 with a core mission to bridge the gap between global financial markets and regional investors. We focus on global financial market research, industry information sorting, asset allocation consulting and cross-border financial business guidance.

We have a senior professional team with rich practical experience in financial field, always adhere to rigorous data verification and objective market judgment, and provide stable and effective financial decision-making reference for all clients.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Uphold the highest ethical standards in all financial advisory services
  • Accuracy: Deliver data-driven insights with zero tolerance for errors
  • Customization: Tailor solutions to meet unique client requirements
  • Transparency: Maintain full openness in all consulting processes and fee structures

Our Global Footprint

While headquartered in the UK, our operational network extends to major financial hubs including New York, Hong Kong, Frankfurt and Singapore, enabling us to provide 24/7 coverage of global markets and real-time response to client needs across different time zones.
